IETE Competition

In the third year of my engineering degree, I led my team in Techno 2019, which is a Maharashtra State Level Project Competition, organized by the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering in association with IETE (Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers). We won the third prize as well as prize money for our project “Face Detection and its applications “.

Robotics



In high school, I was part of the first-ever Robotics Club.

Our team won the Gold medal for standing first in the First Tech Challenge National Robotics Competition.

After this win, our team represented India at the First Tech Challenge Robotics World Championship in St. Louis, Missouri, USA.

Handball



In high school, I was part of the Handball team.

Our team won the following championships. The first prize in under 14 district level sports competition, second prize in under 14 state level sports competition, and the first prize in under 17 district-level sports competition for the sport of Handball. These certificates were awarded by the Government of Maharashtra.

Tug of War




In high school, I was an athlete and participated in many sports activities.

I represented the school in handball and basketball tournaments, and was part of the Tug of War team as well.

My team won the Tug of War competition and we won the gold medal for it.
