Consulting

I am part of the Texas A & M Consulting Group where I serve as a Project Team Lead.
Last semester, I headed a group of undergraduate students on a client engagement, to help a client in the Education Technology industry. The team assisted the client to establish their brand identity through the UI/UX redesign of the website. We analyzed the business model, value proposition, user personas, competition, and current website data to develop a strong brand identity and incorporated findings from the analysis to inform the design and creation of wireframes and mockups for the new website.

Writing

Writing serves as an outlet for my thoughts and feelings. I aspire to publish my own book of poetry one day.
Additionally, I am a writer at the Texas A & M Freelance Writers Association at Texas A&M University. The mission of this organization is to affect positive social change by inviting people to share their perspectives, sharpen their opinions, and participate in meaningful conversations with others surrounding the topics they care about the most.

Volunteering

During high school, I volunteered at K.C. Thackeray - Vidya Niketan English Medium School. Here, I supported the students to improve their academic performance and in their various extracurricular activities. Additionally, I participated in the World Diabetes Day Awareness Drive, which was an initiative by Jehangir Medical Research Institute, which is part of Jehangir Hospital, in Pune.
